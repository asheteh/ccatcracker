CDAC C-CAT Admit Card 2019



CDAC C-CAT Admit Card 2019 The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) released the C-CAT 2019 admit card on December 4, 2019. Candidates who successfully filled the CDAC application form were able to download their CDAC C-CAT admit card 2019 in online mode till December 14, 2019 with the help of their login credentials (CDAC application form number and password). Candidates are required to download and print the CDAC admit card on a clean paper and have to carry the same along on the exam day. Those who do not present their CDAC C-CAT admit card before concerned authorities/invigilators at the exam centre, will not be allowed to sit for the examination. A government-issued identity proof has to be brought with the admit card of CDAC C-CAT exam 2019 to the exam centre. Thus, candidates appearing for CDAC CCAT December 2019 exam are advised to go through the article below for more information on CDAC admit card.

How to download CDAC C-CAT Admit Card 2019?


1. Visit the official website of CDAC at cdac.in

2. Click on the link to ‘Candidate login’.

3. Now enter your form number and password (generated at the time of filling the CDAC C-CAT application form.

4. There will be a link to ‘C-CAT admit card’, click on it.

5. The CDAC C-CAT admit card will be displayed, now download and take 2-3 prints of the same for future reference.

CDAC C-CAT Counselling and seat allocation 2019


After the declaration of CDAC C-CAT result, it is being followed by the counselling process. The counselling of CDAC CCAT 2019 is being conducted in online mode only wherein candidates need their form number and password to lock their choices of courses and training centres. Candidates are being allocated seats strictly according to the order of their preferences and ranks. A candidate who does not get his/ her first preference can try again for the next round of CDAC C-CAT counselling 2019. However, a candidate cannot opt for a lower preferred choice if he/ she bags the higher preference in any round of CDAC C-CAT 2019 counselling .
