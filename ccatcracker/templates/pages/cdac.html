CDAC C-CAT Counselling and seat allocation 2019


After the declaration of CDAC C-CAT result, it is being followed by the counselling process. The counselling of CDAC CCAT 2019 is being conducted in online mode only wherein candidates need their form number and password to lock their choices of courses and training centres. Candidates are being allocated seats strictly according to the order of their preferences and ranks. A candidate who does not get his/ her first preference can try again for the next round of CDAC C-CAT counselling 2019. However, a candidate cannot opt for a lower preferred choice if he/ she bags the higher preference in any round of CDAC C-CAT 2019 counselling .

Candidates will selected on the basis of the ranks based on their performances in Section A, Sections A+B, Sections A+B+C of C-CAT exam . After online choice filling form of their preferences of course and training centre, there will be two counselling process and three seat allotment rounds.

First Counselling



In first counselling according the ranks candidates will be allocated seats in the order of rank and preferences of courses and centres. Two rounds of seat allocations based on thefirst counselling will be done

If the candidate do not have good enough ranks they might not get top preferences course or centres.

However, the seats allotted in the first preference and in the first round to the candidates will be their final allocated seat and there will be no change for them in the second round of seat allocations. And the seats allocated in the second round will be the final allocated and there will be no further change in them.


Second Counselling


In the second counselling, candidates who have not got admission or have not allotted seats have to fill the online choice for the vacant seats in the centres and courses. The second counselling will be scheduled after the payment of the first installment fee of the second round of seat allocation. The third round of seat allocation is based on the second counseling.


Seat Allotment Result


In the both the counselling, CDAC will released the result in the candidates login for the seat allotted to the candidates. Candidates can check the result and the seat allotted to them by logging in with Form number and Password. The decision made by the CDAC and the seats allotted to the candidates in all the rounds are the final seats allotted to them, after that no changes can be made further. After the allocation of seats, candidates have to pay the course fee either in installments or full, online on the official website.
