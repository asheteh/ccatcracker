CDAC C-CAT Result 2020



CDAC C-CAT Result 2019 - The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) has announced the first round of CDAC seat allotment on January 8 for counselling held between Dec 27 and Jan 6, 2020. The allotted candidates can pay the first instalment of fee till January 16, 2020. The authorities had declared Computerized Common Admission Test (C-CAT) result for upcoming session on December 27, 2019 at 6 pm. They released the CDAC C-CAT result 2019 in online mode and candidates are required to login using their application form number and password. The result of CDAC C-CAT 2019 is displayed in the form of rank card which must be downloaded for future reference. Those who appear for more than one section will be assigned multiple ranks. As the CDAC C-CAT exam result is announced, candidates now have to participate in the online counselling round. According to their preferences and ranks secured, candidates will be provided with courses and training centres accordingly. The article below deals with all the aspects related to CDAC C-CAT result 2019.

CDAC C-CAT Counselling and seat allocation 2019

After the declaration of CDAC C-CAT result, it is being followed by the counselling process. The counselling of CDAC CCAT 2019 is being conducted in online mode only wherein candidates need their form number and password to lock their choices of courses and training centres. Candidates are being allocated seats strictly according to the order of their preferences and ranks. A candidate who does not get his/ her first preference can try again for the next round of CDAC C-CAT counselling 2019. However, a candidate cannot opt for a lower preferred choice if he/ she bags the higher preference in any round of CDAC C-CAT 2019 counselling .
