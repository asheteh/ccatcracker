CDAC C-CAT Application Form 2019- The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (CDAC) released the application form for February 2020 batch of Computerized Common Admission Test- C-CAT registration. The authorities commenced the CDAC C-CAT registration for December 2019 on October 30 which lasted till November 27, 2019 and the respective candidates were required to fill the same through official website in online mode only.
It may be noted that CCAT 2019 is the entrance examination for admission to Postgraduate diploma courses offered by various training centres of CDAC. Only those candidates complying with the eligibility criteria, which differs for different PG Diploma courses, are able to proceed with CDAC C-CAT registration and application form respectively. The process includes various steps including payment of application fee, selection of course and CDAC training centre, etc. An application form number will be generated after completion of this process which will be required during CDAC login further along with the password set by candidates.
