Steps to fill CDAC C-CAT Application Form 2019

The process of CDAC registration followed by filling the CDAC C-CAT 2019 Application Form December 2019 is a multi-level process. The steps provided below are to be followed by aspirants:

Visit the official website of CDAC

Follow Below Steps :



Step 1- CDAC C-CAT Registration


The CDAC C-CAT 2019 registration is the first step while applying for the entrance examination. Candidates are required to fill in some generic details. The process will end up with formation of CDAC C-CAT application form number and password. Candidates are advised to note down these two as they are crucial for CDAC login in future.

Step 2- Selection of CDAC Courses


In this step, candidates are required for CDAC login using their form number and password formed at the time of CDAC C-CAT registration . Herein, they have to enter some personal as well as professional details. Candidates are also required to carefully select the category of CDAC PG Diploma courses as well because no second chance will be provided to change the same.

1. Candidates applying for Category 1 are only eligible for PG-DGi.

2. Category 2 candidates are eligible for courses under both category 1 as well as category 2 respectively.

3. Eligible candidates selecting the Category 3 can apply for all PG Diploma courses mentioned in Category 3.

Step 3- Uploading photograph


The next step of CDAC C-CAT application form December 2019 requires the respective candidates to upload their passport size photograph. It must be assured by aspirants that they upload a picture which is clear and their face is visible enough for recognition by concerned authorities.



Step 4- Payment of examination fee


The application fee for CDAC C-CAT application form 2019 varies according to the category of course selected by candidates as well as the computer system opted for, i.e personal laptop or that of examination centre. Hence, the examination fee for CDAC C-CAT 2019 is as follows:

Final- Selection of city and date of C-CAT exam December 2019


After payment of application fee, the candidates have to complete CDAC C-CAT application December 2019 form by selecting the city and date of examination. As C-CAT exam is conducted for two days in a session, candidates have to select the exam day while filling the CDAC C-CAT application form 2019. It may be noted that the availability of a particular exam centre depends upon the capacity therein, and if the seats are filled, then further applicants will not be able to select such of exam cities as it will not show in the drop down menu itself.

After successful completion of CDAC C-CAT application form 2019, candidates are advised to download and take 2-3 prints of the same for future reference.
